Choclate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2-1/4 c all-purpose flour
  • 1 tspn of baking sode
  • 1 tspn salt
  • 2 sticks butter, softened
  • 3/4 c sugar
  • 3/4 c packed brown sugar
  • 1 tspn vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 c semi-sweet choclate chips

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375.

Combine flour, baking soda and salt in smal bowl.

Beat butter, sugar and vanilla in large bowl.

Stir in eggs, one at a time.

Add flour gradually, then stir until almost combined.

Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheet.

Bake 9-11 minutes. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes.
